About us

My name is Titouan Winkel, I am 18 years old and I am the author of this site. I am currently in my first year of a BUT MMI in Montbéliard. This site was created as part of an individual project.

Given the topic "prehistoric future", I decided to focus mainly on the term "future" because I find new technologies and science fiction fascinating.

Having a certain attraction to art in general, I am interested in everything around me. This is why I chose urbanization as a theme and the building as an object.

The building inspires an artistic thought in me because it evokes street art, which I am very interested in, as well as the Bauhaus school, which gave birth to the American glass buildings that we all know. The building is also a place full of life that we do not necessarily pay attention to, which also pushed me to highlight this object on this site.

The video game Cyberpunk 2077 also greatly inspired me for the choice of theme and colors.
