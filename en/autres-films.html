Blade Runner 2049 (2017)

In "Blade Runner 2049", K (Ryan Gosling), a replicant Blade Runner, discovers that a replicant has given birth, which threatens the established order. While searching for this child, K initially believes he is involved, but eventually discovers it is someone else. His investigation leads him to Rick Deckard (Harrison Ford), a former Blade Runner in exile.

Tron (2010)

In "Tron" (1982), Kevin Flynn, a programmer and video game designer, is drawn into a virtual world by the Master Control Program, a tyrannical artificial intelligence. Inside the computer system, Flynn meets Tron, a security program designed to free the network from the MCP's domination. Together, they lead a rebellion to restore freedom in this digital world.

Hunger Games (2012)

In "Hunger Games" (2012), Katniss Everdeen lives in a dystopia where the Capitol strictly controls the 12 districts. Each year, a boy and a girl from each district are chosen to participate in the "Hunger Games", a televised fight to the death. Katniss volunteers to save her sister and enters the arena alongside Peeta. Together, they struggle to survive against deadly opponents and the Capitol's manipulation.

Maze Runner (2014)

In "Maze Runner" (2014), Thomas wakes up amnesiac in the middle of a community of young people trapped in a place called the Glade, surrounded by a gigantic ever-changing maze. None of them know why they are there, but they desperately seek a way out. Thomas, with his new friends, embarks on a quest to understand the mysteries of the maze and find a way to escape.
